ACHIEVEMENTS

  • 06/2021. Compassionate Hearts Award

    In 2021, VR1Family was bestowed with the Compassionate Hearts Award by the Global Humanitarian Foundation, in recognition of their remarkable efforts in providing immediate relief and long-term support to natural disaster survivors. This prestigious award celebrates VR1Family’s unwavering compassion, tireless dedication, and transformative impact on the lives of thousands of individuals and communities in their time of need.

  • 12/2021.Resilience Innovator Prize

    VR1Family was honored with the Resilience Innovator Prize by the International Disaster Resilience Council in 2023. This distinguished accolade acknowledges the organization’s groundbreaking initiatives, including their use of advanced technology, data-driven strategies, and community engagement models that have revolutionized disaster response and recovery efforts worldwide.
